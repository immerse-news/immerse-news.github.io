Q&A: Hacking VR Speakers Vicki Ferguson and Olivier Palmieri of Ubisoft

Immerse on 2017-05-22

What are the best and worst uses of VR for documentary? What excites makers the most about the technology? Vicki Ferguson and Olivier Palmieri of Ubisoft share insights from their work on Eagle Flight, a virtual reality simulation video game released for Microsoft Windows and PlayStation 4.
