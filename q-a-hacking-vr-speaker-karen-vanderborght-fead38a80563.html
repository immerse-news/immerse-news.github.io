Q&A: Hacking VR Speaker Karen Vanderborght

Immerse on 2017-05-22

Why create for an inaccessible medium such as VR? And what does VR need to make it more accessible? Creative Director Karen Vanderborght, from the National Film Board of Canada, discusses her work in designing a visual language for VR. Her latest project, Ocean School, combines classic documentary with 360 video, VR experiences, and games.
