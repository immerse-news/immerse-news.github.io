Q&A: Hacking VR Speaker Arnaud Colinart

Immerse on 2017-04-28

Arnaud Colinart shares his thoughts on the past, present, and future of virtual reality and documentary. Colinart is a producer and creative director at AGAT Films & Cie/EX NIHILO, an award-winning production company based in Paris. His productions include the interactive VR experience Notes On Blindness — Into Darkness which has been shown in many festivals worldwide including Sundance Film Festival 2016, Tribeca Film Festival 2016 and SXSW 2017.
